BALL SURFERS

BALL SURFERS is an cross-platform multiplayer and singleplayer game in which you have to avoid obsticle by moving left or right and you can also compare your highscore with other players. In multiplayer you have different gamemodes with customizable gameplay. The new update includes new gamemodes, new customization options, new achievement system, new save system and update User-Interface
